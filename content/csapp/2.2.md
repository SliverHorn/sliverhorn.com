## Unsigned Encodings

For vector $\vec{x} = [x_{w-1},x_{w-2},...,x_0]$

$$
B2U_{w}(\vec{x})\stackrel{.}{=}x_{w-1}{\cdot}2^{w-1}+x_{w-2}{\cdot}2^{w-2}+...+x_{0}{\cdot}2^{0}=\sum_{i=0}^{w-1}{x_{i}2^{i}}
$$
